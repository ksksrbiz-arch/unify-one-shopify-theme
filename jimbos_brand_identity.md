# Jimbo's Iron & Thread Supply Co. — Brand Identity

**Author**: Manus AI
**Date**: March 05, 2026

## 1. Brand Strategy: Modern Craftsman

Jimbo's Iron & Thread occupies a unique space between heavy industrial supply and fine craftsmanship. The brand identity, **Modern Craftsman**, is designed to bridge this gap. It speaks to the professional who needs durable, reliable tools ("Iron") but also values precision, quality, and a sense of heritage in their work ("Thread").

The brand avoids the purely utilitarian, often dated aesthetic of large industrial suppliers like Grainger, and sidesteps the mass-market, purely fashion-focused feel of some workwear brands. Instead, it targets the discerning modern tradesperson, artisan, and serious hobbyist who sees their work as a craft.

## 2. Brand Voice & Tone

The voice is **confident, expert, and approachable**. It's the voice of a seasoned professional who is passionate about their craft and eager to share their knowledge without being condescending.

- **Confident**: We use declarative statements and stand behind our products.
- **Expert**: We provide detailed product information, how-to guides, and technical specifications.
- **Approachable**: We use clear, concise language, avoiding overly technical jargon where possible. The tone is welcoming and supportive.

## 4. Typography

The typography pairs a strong, industrial-inspired headline font with a highly readable, modern body font.

- **Headings**: **Roboto Condensed** (Bold). This condensed, sans-serif font is strong and space-efficient, evoking the lettering on machinery and industrial signage.
- **Body Text**: **Lato** (Regular). Lato is a clean, friendly, and highly legible sans-serif font that is perfect for product descriptions, articles, and all other body copy.

## 5. Logo Direction

The logo should be a clean, modern mark that visually represents both "Iron" and "Thread".

- **Concept**: A stylized anvil, representing strength and the "Iron" aspect of the business. A single, continuous line forms the anvil and then loops into a stylized needle and thread, representing the "Thread" and craftsmanship aspect.
- **Style**: A minimalist, single-weight line style. The logo should work well in a single color (white on the charcoal background, or charcoal on a light background).
- **Lockup**: The logomark can be paired with the full brand name, "Jimbo's Iron & Thread Supply Co.", set in Roboto Condensed.
